What are we on about here?

National Bureau of Standards Fan Fiction: The US National Bureau of Standards (NBS) was born in 1901. It has held our hands, as a society, through the invention of the automobile, the nuclear bomb, computers and the internet. All images on the Doors site are sourced from public domain NBS reports, and you can find more here. In their spirit of organizing chaos into accessible consensus-supporting content, we made this.

Hope: Writers are meaning-makers, and in a world of endless innovation, creatives are often a sounding board. We don’t just ask what we can do, but why we should do it. The fields of robotics and artificial intelligence are bringing to us new horizons, which bring with them new questions: moral, ethical, psychological. It’s our job to ask these questions, and to use our storytelling minds to try and work together to seek answers.
