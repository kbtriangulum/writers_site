[Non-fiction]

A directory of institutions, some centuries old and others brand new, all with archives of relevant reports that are accessible to the general public. For people who like to track sources and read footnotes.

All images are stored in the assets/images directory.

CRS: Congressional Research Service

Founded: 1914 [orig. Legislative Reference Service, US gov.]

Resources: Summaries of policy on current issues --- Can we legally give robots guns?

Org Bio: CRS provides timely, objective, and authoritative research and analysis for committees and Members of Congress.

Reports Directory: https://example.org/crs-products

Example reports: AI Vocabulary Guide, AI in Healthcare, AI Weapons Systems, AI in Elections

Founded: 2021 [nonprofit, NGO]

Org Bio: The database indexes harms and near-harms so researchers, builders and policy teams can learn from past failures.
